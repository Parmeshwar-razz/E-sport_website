RULES & ELIGIBILITY

01

Eligibility

All participants are required to present a valid College ID card as proof. Minumun age requires: 16 Years.

02

Team Composition

4 compulsory players + 1 optional substitute for BGMI. 5 compulsory players + 1 optional substitute for Valorant and MOBA Legends.

03

Registration

Every registered player must provide complete and accurate details during registration. No changes allowed after registration is completed.

04

Fair Play

Cheating, hacking, or unsportsmanlike conduct leads to immediate disqualification. Organizer decisions are final.

05

Device Policy

Players must bring their own devices. BGMI & MLBB on personal phones. Valorant requires a laptop/PC.

06

Punctuality

All players must be present at check-in with College IDs. Teams failing to check in on 9AM may be forfeited.
